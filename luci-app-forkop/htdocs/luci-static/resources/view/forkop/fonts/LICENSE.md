## Twemoji Country Flags

The `TwemojiCountryFlags.woff2` font is distributed by the
[`country-flag-emoji-polyfill`](https://github.com/talkjs/country-flag-emoji-polyfill)
project. The font is a subset of Twemoji Mozilla containing country flags.

The emoji artwork comes from [Twemoji](https://github.com/twitter/twemoji) and
is redistributed under the
